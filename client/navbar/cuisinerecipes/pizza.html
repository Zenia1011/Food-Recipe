Pizza



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • For mixture
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 tsp garlic butter/oil
  • 6 pods of garlic
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 Capsicum
  • 1 tomato
  • 8 chicken cocktails
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper powder

    • For the base sauce
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 tbsp schezwan chutney
  • 1 tbsp Red chilli sauce
  • 1 tsp pizza seasoning
  • 1 tsp oregano

    • Other Ingredients
  • Cheese


  • METHOD: 
    •           Step 1: Dough
          In a bowl mix all the Ingredients in the dough section and Knead for 15 to 20 min.
          
          Then apply oil to the dough and set it aside for 1hr. 
          
          Then make 2 parts of it


          Step 2: Mixture
          In a pan heat the garlic butter and add the garlic. 
          
          Then add the onions, carrot, Capsicum, tomato, cocktail, soy sauce, salt & pepper powder and let it cook.


          Step 3: Baking
          In a baking tray brush oil and flatten 1 part of the dough into a circle. 
          
          Then with a fork make some holes to avoid the dough to rise. 
          
          Now spread the prepared sauce on the dough and add spread the mixture.
          
          Grate some cheese on top. Sprinkle oregano and pizza seasoning. 
          
          Preheat the oven at 180Â°C and bake it for 20 min.
          
          The second time keep tge pizza for only 12min ti avoid burning. 
          (SAYING THIS BY EXPERIENCE).
