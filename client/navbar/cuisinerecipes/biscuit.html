Biscuit Fudge



  • INGREDIENTS:

  • 18 biscuits (preferred biscuit Marie/parle-G)
  • 1 tsp all purpose flour
  • 1.5 tsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp sugar powder
  • 4 tsp melted butter
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Dry fruits of ur choice (here I have used raisins soaked in rum)


  • METHOD: 
    •           Step 1: BLENDING
          In a mixer grind the biscuits. Once done transfer it to a plate.

          STEP 2: MIXING
          To the powdered biscuit add all purpose flour, sugar powder and mix.
          
          Add melted butter and mix it thoroughly.
          
          Add the milk and make a dough.
          
          Flatten the dough and add the dry fruits and roll the dough.
          
          Wrap it nicely in a butter paper/foil and refrigerate for 30 mins.
          
          Cut into desired shapes..
