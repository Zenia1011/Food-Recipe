Baklawa



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • For dough
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 3/4 cup + 5tbsp
  • Chopped Dry fruits of ur choice

    • For sugar syrup
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 and 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp jaggery powder
  • 1.5 inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp cardamon powder / 3-4 whole cardamom



  • METHOD: 

    •           STEP 1: DOUGH-
          In a bowl mix all the ingredients mentioned in the dough section and let it set for 15mins.
    
          
          
          Step 2: SUGAR SYRUP-
          In a container add all the ingredients in the sugar syrup section and let it cook for 8 mins on medium flame and bring it to a boil.
    
          

          Step 3: ASSEMBLING-
          Make 4 portions of the dough and roll each of them.
          
          Then on the 1st layer sprinkle all purpose flour and apply melted butter and 
    then place the 2nd layer on top.
          
          Repeat for all 3 layers.
          
          Once done roll it till it forms a thin layer.
          
          Then put the chopped dry fruits in the centre of the 
     dough.
          
          Cover it from both sides.
    
          
          NOTE: ALL THE ROLLED DOUGH SHOULD BE THIN.
    
          

          Step 4: BAKING
          Preheat the oven at 180Â°C.
          
          Bake for 25 mins.
    
          

          Step 5: COOLING
          Add the baked baklava to the sugar syrup and let it cool in the fridge over night or for 6-7 hrs.
