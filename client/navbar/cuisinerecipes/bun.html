Stuffed Buns



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • For The Dough
  • Maida 3 cups
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • Sugar 1 & 1/2 tsp
  • Yeast 2 & 1/2 tsp
  • 1 Egg
  • Warm Milk 1/4th tsp
  • Oil 1 tbsp
  • Sesame Seeds for garnishing
    • Let it rest for 2 hrs

    For The Filling
  • Use any vegies of your choice
