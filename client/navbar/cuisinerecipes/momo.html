Momos



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • For the dough
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp oil
  • Let it sit for 15 min

    • For the filling
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 capcicum
  • 1 onion
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic
    • (Add more vegies if required)

    For the momo chutney
  • 3 kashmir chillies
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp soya sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • Corriander


  • METHOD: 
    •     In a pan heat oil garlic and all the veggies add salt and 1 tsp pepper powder and 1 tsp soy sauce 
    and saute it for 5 to 10 mins.

    Once the dough is set make small portions and roll it. 
    
    Place some mixture in each of those portions and make any desired shape. 
    
    Place them all on a tray and steam them for abt 1 hr on low flame. 

    KEEP FOR LONGER TIME IF REQUIRED. 
    
    Remove when they become slightly transparent

    Grind 2 tomatoes and 3 red chillies
    
    In a pan heat oil and fry the garlic add the tomatoe and chilli puree and add the remaining ingredients 
    mentioned in the momo chutney para. 
    
    Garnish with corriander.

    Add more chillies if ull like spicy.
