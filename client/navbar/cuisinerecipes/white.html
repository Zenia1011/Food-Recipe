White Chocolate Fudge



  • INGREDIENTS:
  • 1 cup milk
  • 100 grams white chocolate compound
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 3 Tsp Sugar


  • METHOD: 
    •           In a non-stick pan heat milk, add the sugar and stir till the milk starts thickening. 
          
          Then transfer the mixture to a bowl and using double boiler add the chocolate to the 
          mixture and let it melt. 
          
          Then add the butter. 
          
          Then in another bowl put butter paper and transfer the mixture and let keep it in the 
          refrigerator till it forms.
