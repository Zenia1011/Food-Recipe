Schezwan Garlic Rice



  • INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cups boiled rice
  • 1 green chilli
  • 2 sliced onions
  • 10 pods of garlic
  • 1 sliced capsicum
  • 1 spring onion
  • 2 tsps chilli flakes
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 2 tsps soy sauce
  • 1.5 tsps ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 4 tbsps Schezwan chutney
  • 5 tbsps oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • METHOD: 
    •           In a pan heat oil and butter..
          
          Then add garlic, green chilli and chilli flakes, ginger garlic paste and saute.
          
          Add the onions and let them cook..
          
          Add the capsicum and saute
          
          Then add the sauces and Schezwan chutney..
          
          Let it cook on low flame for 5mins

          Then add the boiled rice and cook for 10 mins on low flame..
