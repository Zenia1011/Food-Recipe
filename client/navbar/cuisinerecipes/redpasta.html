Red Sauce Pasta



  • INGREDIENTS:

  • 3 cups macroni
  • Water for boiling
  • 1 Big Onions cut in slice
  • 1 Red Bell peppper (Capsicum)(optional)
  • 1 Yellow Bell Pepper(Capsicum)(optional)
  • 200 gms mushroom
  • 1 Green Bell pepper
  • 3-4 spring onions
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp Garlic butter
  • 22 pods of garlic
  • 3 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 2 tsp ketchup
  • 4 tsp red chilli sauce
  • 3 tsp shezwan chutney
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1.5 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • Salt as per taste
  • 1 tsp pizza seasoning (optional)
  • 1 tsp oregano

    • For Garnishing:
  • 1/4 tsp spring onion
  • 1/4 tsp sesame seeds


  • METHOD: 
    •            STEP 1: Boiling
          In a container boil water and add the macroni to it.Add salt and 2 tsp oil.
          
          Once the macroni is boiled drain tge water and set it aside.


          STEP 2: Puree
          In a mixer slice 2 tomatoes. Add a pinch of red food cour. 
          
          Add salt and little soy sauce and make a puree.


          STEP 3: Sauce
          In a pan heat garlic butter add the chilli, garlic and saute. 
          
          Then add the onions and let it cook. 
          
          Then add the vegetables and mushrrom and saute. 
          
          Then add the sauces and tomato puree and cover the lid and let it cook for 10 min on low flame.


          STEP 4: Mixing
          Mix the boiled macroni and tge prepared sauce (step 2).
          
          Cover the lid and let it cook for 30min on low flame.

          Transfer to a plate and garnish with sesame seeds and spring onions
