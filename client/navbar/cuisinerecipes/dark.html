Dark Chocolate



  • INGREDIENTS:
  • 1 cup Cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar powder (icing sugar)
  • 1/2 tbsp all purpose flour (maida)
  • Salt a pinch
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup Cream
    • NOTE: IF UR NOT HAVING CREAM
    U CAN SUBSTITUTE IT BY MAKING A MIXTURE
    OF 6tbsp milk + 2 tbsp butter...

  • METHOD: 
    •                     In a mixing bowl mix all the dry ingredients mentioned above.

          Next heat a large container. Add butter. Let it melt. Once it melts add milk and stir.  
          
    
          Add vanilla essence and cream and stir it.
          
          Add all the dry ingredients and mix it thoroughly. Let it thicken.
          
          Don't allow the sides to burn.
     Once it thickens transfer the mixture in a mould.
          
          Let it cool down and form.

          NOTE: IF THE CHOCOLATES DOES NOT FORM THEN KEEP IT IN THE FREEZER FOR SOME TIME AND THEN KEEP IT 
          BACK IN THE FRIDGE..

          NOTE: IF U WANT U CAN ADD DRYFRUITS IN THE CHOCOLATE....HERE I HAVE USED BLACK RAISINS AND SOME 
          OTHER DRY FRUITS...

          NOTE: IF U DONT HAVE A MOULD THEN BRUSH ANY CONTAINER WITH BUTTER/OIL AND TRANSFER THE MIXTURE IN IT..
          ONCE IT 
     FORMS CUT INTO DESIRED SHAPES...
