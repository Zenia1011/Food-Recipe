Cocktail Chillifry



INGREDIENTS:
  • 10 cocktails
  • 10 pics of garlic
  • 2 green chillies slit
  • 2 medium onions
  • 1 capsicum
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 potato
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp coriander (garnish)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala powder
  • Salt as per taste


  • METHOD: 
    •   In a wok heat oil.

  Add the chillies and garlic.
  
  Add the onions and let it get golden brown.

  Add the carrot and capsicum and saute.
    
  
  Add the potatoes and cover the lid for 5 mins.

  Add the powders, sauces salt and sugar and saute.

  Cover the lid for 5 mins.

  Add the 
     cocktails and saute.

  Cover the lid for 5mins.

  Garnish with coriander.
