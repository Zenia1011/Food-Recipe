Hot & Sweet Soya Chunks



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • For marination :
  • 1 cup Soya chunks (After soaking for 10 min)
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  • 1 tbsp all purpose flour (maida)
  • 1.5 tbsp corn flour
  • 1.5 tsp red chilli sauce
  • Oil for deep frying

    • Other Ingredients :
  • 2 Onions cut in cubes
  • 3 tbsp Oil
  • 5-6 pods of garlic
  • 1.5 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp jaggery powder
  • 1 tsp ketchup
  • 2 tsp red chilli sauce
  • 2 tsp shezwan chutney
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • Salt as per taste
  • 1 tsp pizza seasoning (optional)
  • 1 tsp oregano

    • For Garnishing :
  • 1/4 tsp corriander / spring onion
  • 1/4 tsp sesame seeds


  • METHOD: 
    •           STEP 1: Soaking
          In a bowl add 1 cup soya chunks.
          
          Add sufficient water and let it soak for 15-20 min.
          
          Then drain the water squeze the chunks to remove excess water.
    


          STEP 2: Marination
          In the bowl now add 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, corn flour, all purpose flour, pepper powder, 
          1 tsp chilli sauce & salt.
          
          Let it rest for 10 min.


          STEP 3: Deep Fry
          In a container heat oil for deep frying.
          
          Then add the soya chunks and deep fry it till it becomes golden brown.
          
          Transfer it to a plate.

          STEP 4: Cooking
          In a wok heat 3 tbsp oil
          
          Add the garlic & green chilli & fry.
          
          Then add the onions and fry it nicely.
          
          Then add 1 tsp red chilli sauce, soy sauce, shezwan chutney, ketchup, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, salt, 
          saute & let it cook.
          
          Then add the deep fried soya chunks
          
          Add pizza seasoning, oregano & saute.
          
          Cover the lid and let it cook for 10 min on low flame.
          
          After 10 mins open the lid and increase the flame and saute for 30 seconds.
          
          Turn the flame off.
          
          Garnish with sesame seeds & corriander.
