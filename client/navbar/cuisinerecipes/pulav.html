Sausage Pulav



  • INGREDIENTS:

  • 25-30 boiled sausages
  • 10 cloves
  • 12 pepper
  • 5 elaichi
  • 1/4 tsp haldi powder
  • 2 cups rice
  • 4 cups hot water
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 onions
  • 1 tomato


  • METHOD: 
    •           In a vessel heat oil.
          
          Add the spices and add the onions and saute.
          
          Then add the tomatoes and haldi powder and saute.
          
          Then add the boiled sausages and cover the lid and let it cook for 10 mins.
          
          Then add the rice and mix.
          
          Then add the hot water and close the lid and let it cook till the water dries up.
