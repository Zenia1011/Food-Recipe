Spaghetti



  • INGREDIENTS:

  • Spaghetti 500 gms
  • oil
  • 7 nos Garlic
  • 3 scallions
  • 2 Tomatoes, 2 tbsp Ketchup, salt
  • 7-8 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp Red chilli sauce
  • 3 tbsp Green chilli sauce
  • 6 tbsp Shezwan chutney
  • 4 tsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 5 tbsp Ketchup
  • 2 tsp Honey
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Pepper powder
  • 1 tbsp Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Sesame seeds
  • Salt as per taste
  • METHOD: 

    •     In a bowl mix soy sauce, honey, Oregano, red chilli sauce, Green chilli sauce, schezwan chutney, 
  pepper powder, ketchup & a pinch of salt. 
  (From the above mentioned ingredients)

  ( Add more Sauces if needed )


  In A Pan
  Heat 2 tbsp oil add garlic, scallions and fry till it gets aromatic.
  
  Then add the prawns and cook it for abt 5 mins.
  
  Then add the tomato puree and the soy sauce mixture and cover and cook for 10 mins.


  In Anothner Container
  Heat water and add oil and salt as per taste and let it boil.
  
  Once the water boils add the Spaghetti.
  
  Drain the water once the Spaghetti gets cooked.
  
  Add 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp pepper powder, oil and salt.
  
  Then add the noodles to the mixture and mix it nicely.
  
  ( add salt and sugar if required)


  Cook it for 30 min on low flame.
  
  Top it with some sesame seeds amd scallions.
