Paneer Manchurian



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • Finely Chopped
  • Ginger 1/4 th inch
  • Garlic 7 pods
  • 1 scallion
  • 1 big green chilli

    • Cube sliced
  • 259 gms paneer
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 Capsicum

    • Other Ingredients
  • 3 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp red chilli sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • water
  • 2 tbsp oil


  • METHOD: 
    •     In a bowl add all purpose flour, cornflour, red chilli powder, salt and water and make a batter.
    
    Add the paneer cubes and mix.
    
    Deep fry the paneer and set aside.

    In a pan heat 2 tbsp oil add garlic, ginger, green chilli and white onions and fry till it turns aromatic.
    
    Then add the onions and fry till the onions get cooked.
    
    Then add the capsicum and mix.
    
    Add tomato sauce, red chilli sauce, soy sauce, sugar and vinegar.
    
    After 1 min add the fried paneer and the green part of the scallion.
