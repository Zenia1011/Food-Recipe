Zingy Parcel



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • For the dough
  • Maida 2 cups
  • Sugar 1 tsp
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • Yeast 2 tsp
  • Water to Knead

    • For the mixture
  • Finely sliced
  • 7-8 pods ofGarlic
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Capsicum
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Tomato
  • 2 Spring onions

    • Other Ingredients
  • Oregano 1 tsp +1/2
  • Pizza seasoning 1 tsp + 1/2 (optional)
  • Oil 3 tbsp + 1
  • Ketchup 3tbsp
  • Soy sauce 1 tbsp
  • Shezwan chutney 2 tbsp
  • Cheese 1 slice
  • Egg 1 (optional)


  • METHOD: 
    •     Mix the ingredients mentioned in the dough section.
    
    Once done tuck the dough and brush 1 tbsp oil on a dough and cover it tightly and let it rest 
    for 2 hrs.

    In a pan heat 2tbp oil and fry the ingredients mentioned in the mixture section and other 
    ingredients section.

    Once the dough is done...divide into 6 parts.
    
    Now take 1 part and roll it fold it from 3 sides and add the mixture in the centre and add cheese.
    
    Fold the 3 sides as shown in the pic.
    
    Use water so that it doesn't leave.
    
    Brush oil and sprinkle oregano and pizza seasoning on top.
