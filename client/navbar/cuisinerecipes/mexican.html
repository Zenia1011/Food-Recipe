Mexican Red Rice



  • INGREDIENTS:

  • 5 tbsp oil
  • 2 cups rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 big capcicum
  • 1 big carrot
  • 2 medium onions
    • (+)any vegies of ur choice
  • 7-8 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup tomato puree/sauce
  • 1-1/2 tbsp garam masala powder
  • Salt as per taste


  • METHOD: 
    •           In a container heat oil, add the washed rice and fry until it becomes golden brown.

          Then add onions, garlic, tomato puree and mix.

          Add vegetables and salt and saute well.

          Then add water and allow the rice to cook.
