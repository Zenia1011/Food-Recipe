Aam Panna

  • INGREDIENTS:
  • water
  • sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 3 medium Green Mangoes
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp pepper powder
  • mint leaves for garnishing

  • METHOD: 
    •         In a cooker heat oil.

          Add the mangoes and cover it and cook for 10 mins on low flame.

          Remove 3 whistles.

          Once done, let the mangoes cool.
    

          Strain and save the pressure cooked water for later.

          Once mangoes are cooled, remove the skin and take out the pulp in a container.
    

          Discard the skin and the seed.

          In a blender, add the mango pulp, cardamom powder, salt, cumin powder, pepper powder.

          Blend it till smooth.
    

          Add 3 tbsp of aam panna in each glass and add saved water and mix well.

          Add ice cubes & garnish with mint leaves.

          Serve Chilled.
