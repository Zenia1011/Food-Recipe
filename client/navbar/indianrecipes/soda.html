Masala Soda



  • INGREDIENTS:

  • Soda
  • Ice cubes
  • 7 mint leaves
  • 1 tsp chopped ginger
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp chat masala
  • 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup water



  • METHOD: 

    •         Add all the ingredients except soda and ice cubes  and blend them to a paste.

          Strain the mixture through a fine strainer.

          Add 5 tbsp of mixture in each glass.

          Fill the glass with club soda.

          Add some ice cubes.

          Garnish with lime slices and mint leaves and serveÂ immediately.
