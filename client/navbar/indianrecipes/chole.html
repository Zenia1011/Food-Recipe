Chole

  • INGREDIENTS:
  • 1 cup boiled chikpeas
  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • 2 medium-sized tomatoes
  • 2 green chilies
  • 4 to 5 medium-sized garlic cloves
  • 1 inch ginger

  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 large tej patta (bay leaf)
  • 2 inches cinnamon
  • 2 to 3 cloves
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 2 to 3 black peppercorns

  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • ¼ teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 pinch asafoetida (hing)
  • ½ or 1 teaspoon dry mango powder (amchur powder)
  • Garam Masala powder


  • METHOD: 
    •         In  a mixer add the onion, tomatoes, 1 green chilli, garlic & ginger and blend it
          make a fine paste

          in a pan heat oil, add cumin seeds, tej patta, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom & pepper
          fry the spices till they become slight fragrant

          Then add the ground paste
          Mix well

          Add turmeric, chilli pwdr, corriander pwdr, hing and amchur powder and saute for a min

          Then add the cooked chikpeas and mix

          Pour abt 1 to 1.5 cups of warm water as required
          Add salt if necessary

          Cover and cook for 12 mins on low flame
          Stir ocassionally
          Add 1 slit green chili and 1 teaspoon garam masala powder or chole masala powder.

          Keep stiring for 8-10mins till the oil starts to leave.

          Add 1 green chilli and garam masala pwdr and stir.
          Add masalas if needed !!
