Matar Paneer

  • INGREDIENTS:
  • 1kg paneer
  • 30 cashews soaked for an hr
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp corriander powder
  • 6 tomatoes
  • 2 medium onions
  • 1 green chilli
  • 10 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1 tbsp curd
  • 3 tbsp ghee
  • Corriander
  • Salt to taste
  • METHOD: 
    •         In a mixer grind the tomatoes, cashews, corriander powder, turmeric powder & chilli powder to fine paste and 
          set it aside.

          In a container heat ghee. 

          Add the garlic and chillies. 

          Then add the onions and let it cook. 

          Then add garam masala powder, salt and saute.

          Then add the paste, curd and peas and add 6tbsp of water and mix and let it cook on low flame for 10 min.

          Then add the paneer and mix gently. 

          Add the corriander and cover the lid and let it cook for 10 to 15 min on low flame. 


          SERVE HOT
