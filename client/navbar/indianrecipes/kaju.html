Kaju Katli



  • INGREDIENTS:

    • Sugar syrup:
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 3/4 Cup sugar

    • Grind:
  • 1 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 & 1/2 cup kaju powder

    • Other Ingredients:
  • 2 tsp ghee
  • Parchment paper
  • Roller


  • METHOD: 

    •         In a pan heat the water and sugar and let the sugar dissolve completely.
        Once done add the kaju powder and stir continuously.
        Add the cardamom powder and stir.
        Once it starts forming a dough add in the ghee in 2 batches and mix till it forms a dough.
        Put the flame off.
        Apply ghee on the parchment paper and transfer the dough on it fold the paper and roll it with a roller.
        Once cooled down make pieces and enjoyy.
  
        NOTE
        THE ENTIRE PROCESS SHOULD BE COOKED ON LOW FLAME
