Jal Jeera



  • INGREDIENTS:

  • 10-15 chopped mint leaves
  • 6 tbsp lemon juice
  • salt
  • 8 cups water
  • 2 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp ginger
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • pinch of asafoetida
  • 2 tbsp tamarind (without seeds)
  • Ice cubes



  • METHOD: 

    •         In a mixer, blend mint leaves, coriander leaves, tamarind, cumin seeds, ginger, asafoetida, salt, sugar and 
          lemon juice.

          Add 4 cups of water in the mixer  and blend the mixture together.

          Strain the mixture in a bowl through a fine strainer.

          Discard the remaining rough part.

          Pour the mixture equally in glasses.

          Pour the remaining 4 glasses of water equally in the glasses.

          Add the ice cubes and serve immediately.
