Gulab Jamun

  • INGREDIENTS:

    • For Dough:
  • 2 cups milk powder
  • 2 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp semolina soaked in 5tbsp
    of water for 10 min
  • 2 tbsp butter

    • For sugar syrup:
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 & 3/4 cup water
  • 4 cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice/vinegar

    • Other ingredients:
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 2 tbsp jaggery power

  • METHOD: 
    •         In a bowl mix all the ingredients mentioned in the dough section. 

          Knead a soft dough. Once the dough is ready set it 
     aside for 10min. 
      
          After 10min make small balls and deep fry them till the get golden brown.
      
          Heat water and add sugar 
     and the rest of ingredients in the sugar syrup section. 

          Bring it to 1 boil, then add jaggery powder and turn the flame off.
    
      
          Mix the deep fried balls in the sugar syrup and cover and keep it for 20-30min aside. 
          
          Once done serve and enjoy...
