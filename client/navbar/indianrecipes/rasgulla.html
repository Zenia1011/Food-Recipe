Rasgulla



  • INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 3 cardamoms



  • METHOD: 
    •         Heat a wok.
          
          Add the milk and let it boil on medium flame.
          
          Reduce the flame once the milk comes to a boil.
          
          Mix lemon juice with 2tbsp water in a bowl.
          
          Gently add the mixture into the milk.
          
          The milk will start to curdle.
          
          Once the milk curdles up completely, turn off the flame.
          
          Sqeeze the Curdled milk with the help of the cloth
          
          Pour 2-3 glasses of water in it and continue sqeezing till the water is drained.
          
          Hang the cloth for 1 hr so tht it drains completely.
          
          Open the cloth and transfer the mixture in a plate.
          
          
          Knead it to form a dough.
          
          Make small balls.
          
          In a container heat cup water then add the sugar and let the sugar dissolve.
          
          Then add the jaggery powder.
          
          Once it starts boiling add the balls slowly.
          
          Cover and cook on medium flame for 5 minutes.
          
          Remove the lid and gently start stiring.
          
          Turn off the flame.
          
          Let it cool overnight.
