Prawn Garlic Manchurian




  • INGREDIENTS:
  • 200 gms prawns
  • 2 small onions cut in cube
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 medium capsicum cut in cube
  • 10-12 pods of garlic
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli flakes
  • 2 green chillies
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 tsp ketchup
  • 2 tsp Schezwan chutney
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice flour
  • 2 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Sesame seeds and spring onion for garnish


  • METHOD: 
    •     STEP 1: Marination
    In a bowl add the prawns, red chilli powder & garam masala powder and mix.
    
    Then in a container mix the rice powder, all purpose flour and water and mix. Add abt 1/2 tsp of chilli powder and a pinch of salt and mix.
    
    Add the prawns.
    
    And let it marinate for 45 mins.


    Step 2: Deep Fry
    In a wok heat oil and then add the marinated prawns and fry them till they turn golden brown.


    Step 3: Manchurian Making
    In a container heat 5 tbsp oil.
    
    Add the garlic and chilli.
    
    Add the ginger garlic paste and chilli flakes and mix.
    
    
    Then add the onions, carrot, capsicum and saute.
    
    Then add the sauces and powders and mix.
    
    Then add a slurry of 1 tsp corn flour and 1 tsp water.
    
    Then add the prawns and let it cook for 10 mins kn low flame.
