Squid Masala




  • INGREDIENTS:

  • 200 grams squids
  • 10 pods garlic
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 2 onions
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 Capsicum
  • 3 green chillies
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 5 kashimiri chillies
  • 6 tbsp ketchup
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp garam masala powder
  • 2 tbsp jaggery powder/sugar
  • 1 tbsp Shezwan chutney
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Sesame seeds for garnishing

  • METHOD: 
    •           Step 1 : Grindd
          In a mixer, grind 2 tomatoes, kashimiri chillies, 1 green chilly, soy sauce, 3 tbsp ketchup to a paste..

          
          Step 2 : Cooking
          In a wok heat oil and butter.
          
          Add the garlic, green chillies and chilly powder..
          
          Add the ginger garlic paste and onion.....
          
          Add the capsicums and saute...
          
          Add the prepared paste mentioned in STEP 1..
          
          Add the ketchup, garam masala powder, schezwan chutney, jaggery powder and salt..
          
          Add the squids and cook for 15mins on low flame...
          
          Add the spring onions...
