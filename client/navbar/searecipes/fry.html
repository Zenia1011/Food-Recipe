Prawn Fry

  • INGREDIENTS:

    • MARINATION
  • 500 gms prawns
  • 2 tbsps red chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 2 tsps soy sauce
  • Salt as per taste
  • 1 tsp meat masala (optional)

    • COATING
  • 3 tbsps all purpose flour (maida)
  • 3 tbsps semolina (rawa)
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • Pinch of salt

    • FRYING
  • 6 tbsps oil


  • METHOD: 
    •          Mix all the ingredients in the marination part and let it rest for 30mins.
        
        Then coat them with the ingredients mentioned in the coating part.
        
        Then in a pan heat 3 tbsps oil and add the prawns.
        
        Let it fry for 5 mins on low flame.
        
        Then flip them and add the remaining 3 tbsps oil and let it fry for 5 mins.
