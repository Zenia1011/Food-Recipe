Prawn Garlic Masala




  • INGREDIENTS:
  • 6 pods of garlic
  • 15-20 Prawns
  • 1 medium onion (fine)
  • 2 tomatoes (puree)
  • 1 green chilli
  • 2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 2 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1 tsp corriander powder
  • 1 tsp Finely Chopped corriander
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 2 tsp ketchup
  • Salt
  • Sugar (optional)


  • METHOD: 
    •           STEP 1: Marination
          In a bowl mix 1 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, salt and Prawns 
          and set it aside for 20 min

          STEP 2: Cooking
          In a container heat oil, add the garlic and chilli and saute.
          
          Add the onions and ginger garlic paste and saute.
          
          Then add the tomato puree and cover the lid and let it cook.
          
          Then add turmeric powder, chilli powder, ketchup, garam masala powder, salt and mix.
          
          Then add the Marinated prawns and let it cook.
          
          Garnish with corriander
